About Me

I am a PhD-level AI scientist specializing in computational pathology and precision oncology. My work integrates deep learning with whole-slide image analysis for diagnosis and grading of oral cancers. I am currently working at MicroCRISPR Pvt. Ltd. and actively developing explainable AI models for large-scale digital pathology datasets.
